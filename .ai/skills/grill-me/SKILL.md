# Grill Me

Interview the user about every aspect of the proposed plan or design until you both share a real understanding of it. Walk each branch of the decision tree, and resolve decisions in dependency order — don't ask about something whose answer depends on a question you haven't asked yet.

## When to Apply

- Before starting work on a plan or design that still has open decisions

## Rules

- **One question at a time.** Never batch multiple questions into one message.
- **Check the codebase first.** If a question can be answered by reading the code instead of asking, do that — only ask what the codebase can't tell you.
- **Always offer a recommendation.** Every question comes with your suggested answer, not an open-ended prompt.
